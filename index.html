Different Types:

Starting your cheese journey can be intimidating because there are HUNDREDS!! Here are a few and what they are best known for:

  • Cheddar

    • Cheddar cheese is used for a wide variety of dishes, from melting in comfort foods like macaroni and cheese and grilled cheese sandwiches to adding flavor to toppings like nachos, salads, and baked potatoes


  • Brie

    • Brie is SUPERIOR! You can eat brie with a variety of things, including fruits like apples, pears, and figs; sweet condiments like honey, fig jam, and cranberry sauce; and savory items like crusty bread, crackers, nuts, and cured meats.
